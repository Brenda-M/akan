The Akan Name Generator !!!!

Hello there! This is a web application that takes your birthday details and then outputs your Akan name depending on your gender. Akan names originate from the Akan people of Ghana who have a unique naming system that is based on the day of the week that one was born and their gender. Read more about the Akan people here

If you would like to know your Akan name, please enter your birthday details and gender below and click submit.
